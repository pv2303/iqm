I have some doubts about which indicator is most appropriate for ODS 1.3. I found two separate datasets -one covering all CadUnico records, categorized by per capita income, and the other including only Bolsa Familia beneficiaries. 
The indicator aims to calculate the percentage between number of people with per capita income below the poverty and the total number of people.

Three options emerge:
- Total of people of CadUnico below the line of poverty divided by the total of people of CadUnico
- Total of Bolsa Familia's beneficiaries below the line of poverty divided by the total of people of CadUnico
- Total of Bolsa Familia's beneficiaries below the line of porverty divided by the total of people of CadUnico

The trick part of this ODS is that its states **Percentual da população abaixo da linha de pobreza após o Bolsa Família**. I assumed  its referring to *after the BF's beneficiaries receive its cash transfers*. Futhermore, I assumed that **total population** would be the population on CadUnico because we do not have records of total population for each municipality for each month of each year (maybe we can use the yearly estimates from IBGE to account the whole population, but then the numerator has to be from december of the respective year. That would be idea 4). 
Finally, as the indicator clearly states **after Bolsa Familia**, I searched a database where this difference is accounted for and I only found data from 2023 and 2024.
